I. Statement

Capital, held with patience—and put to work.

01Long-term horizon
02Privately held
03No public capital
04No third-party clients
05Limited public presence

Damask Holdings is a private holdings company. We acquire, operate, and build businesses and assets across a small number of focus areas.

We take a long-term view. We acquire what we understand, improve what we acquire, and part with it only when the timing is right.

The work is deliberate. We acquire selectively, improve steadily, and sell when it makes sense.

What we own, we build. The rest is detail.
